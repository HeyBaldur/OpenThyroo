About Thyroo

Thyroo is a web application that connects business people, software developers, architects, or any other professional by a strong algorithm avoiding wrong connections, chatting or messaging with people who do not have anything in common with. Thyroo saves you time when you need to search for people to make your business grow faster or develop your project or simply share an idea and build a team to make it come true. In the future, Thyroo will introduce a portal job but as a second face of the project.

Our Vision

Our vision is connecting effortlessly with the right people and create the right business/economic opportunities for anyone.

Who are we?

Thyroo began in founders Rodolfo A. Calvo's & Michael A. Campos living room in Jan of 2020's and was officially launched on Sep 5, 2020, under the pressure of the COVID-19 health situation in 2020.
